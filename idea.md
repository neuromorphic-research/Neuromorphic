My startup is called Neuromorphic. We build camera systems and proprietry vlm models to: We are building easy-to-integrate camera systems that run vision-language models in real time, allowing robots to understand not just what they see, but what it means. Instead of treating cameras as passive sensors, we turn them into semantic perception modules that can identify objects, interpret scenes, and provide actionable context directly to robotic systems.

We are building this because robotics still struggles with perception in unstructured real-world environments. Traditional vision systems are often brittle, narrow, and expensive to adapt for each use case. By making real-time semantic understanding plug-and-play, we help robotics companies deploy smarter systems faster, with less integration overhead and far more flexibility.
