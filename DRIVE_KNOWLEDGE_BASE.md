## Drive Hygiene Rules

- Keep Neuromorphic company materials under the `Neuromorphic` Drive folder.
- Use the numbered folders for durable organization. Do not create new top-level categories unless the existing ones do not fit.
- Place demo, pilot, robot-evaluation, and video-script work under `04 Demos and Robots`.
- Place website, logo, brand, and public narrative work under `06 Brand and Marketing`.
- Place pitch decks, accelerator applications, investor lists, and funding docs under `07 Fundraising and Accelerators`.
- Place customer/pilot letters and relationship tracking under `05 Sales and Partnerships`.
- Use `99 Archive` for superseded docs instead of deleting.
- Before creating a new file, search the Neuromorphic folder for an existing doc with the same purpose.
